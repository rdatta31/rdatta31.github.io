RISHABH DATTA

Rishabh Datta is a graduate student in Mechanical Engineering at Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Rishabh is interested in magnetohydrodynamic turbulence in pulsed-power driven high energy density laboratory plasmas. Rishabh is also interested in Sustainability, and is a co-char for MIT's Graduate Student Council Sustainability.

My 2020 Reading List

This year, I made a deliberate effort to read more diversely. In particular, I'm happy about the increase in the number of fiction books I read this year. I also tried to read genres I typically do not read about. I went back and re-read some old favorites, and experimented with audiobooks this year. Below is a list of the books I read this year.
